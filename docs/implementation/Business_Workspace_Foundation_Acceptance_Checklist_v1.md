\- Founder  
\- Mission Control

to validate that the implementation complies with:

\- Business Workspace Foundation Build Contract

\# Acceptance Criteria

\#\# 1\. Foundation Preservation

Verify:

\- ☐ Public website remains unchanged.  
\- ☐ Authentication behaves exactly as previously accepted.  
\- ☐ Protected routing remains unchanged.  
\- ☐ Session persistence remains unchanged.  
\- ☐ Session restoration remains unchanged.  
\- ☐ Business Identity Foundation remains fully functional.  
\- ☐ Existing Business Identity data is preserved.

\*\*Result\*\*

☐ PASS

\---

\#\# 2\. Business Workspace Home

Verify:

\- ☐ Existing \`/dashboard\` workspace refined rather than replaced.  
\- ☐ No duplicate workspace created.  
\- ☐ Existing Business Identity displayed correctly.  
\- ☐ Business Name displayed correctly.  
\- ☐ Business Category displayed exactly as stored.  
\- ☐ Business Locality/Address displayed exactly as stored.  
\- ☐ Business Identity remains read-only.

\*\*Result\*\*

☐ PASS

\---

\#\# 3\. Workspace Organization

Verify:

\- ☐ Workspace contains clear organizational sections.  
\- ☐ Workspace feels organized and uncluttered.  
\- ☐ Business Identity section is clearly identifiable.  
\- ☐ Orientation section is present.  
\- ☐ Future capability section is present.  
\- ☐ Existing layout preserved where appropriate.

\*\*Result\*\*

☐ PASS

\---

\#\# 4\. Orientation Experience

Verify:

\- ☐ Welcome message is present.  
\- ☐ Orientation messaging is reassuring.  
\- ☐ Messaging reflects Smart Business philosophy.  
\- ☐ No error-like states presented.  
\- ☐ No unnecessary instructional complexity introduced.

\*\*Result\*\*

☐ PASS

\---

\#\# 5\. Future Capability Placeholders

Verify:

\- ☐ Future capabilities clearly identified as "Coming Soon".  
\- ☐ Placeholders are informational only.  
\- ☐ No operational functionality implemented.  
\- ☐ No sample business data displayed.  
\- ☐ No dashboard metrics displayed.  
\- ☐ No zero-value KPI cards displayed.  
\- ☐ No simulated AI responses displayed.  
\- ☐ No misleading interactive behaviour.

\*\*Result\*\*

☐ PASS

\---

\#\# 6\. Business Identity Integrity

Verify:

\- ☐ Business Name unchanged.  
\- ☐ Business Category unchanged.  
\- ☐ Business Locality unchanged.  
\- ☐ No editing capability introduced.  
\- ☐ No duplicate Business Identity records created.  
\- ☐ Existing Business Identity behaviour preserved.

\*\*Result\*\*

☐ PASS

\---

\#\# 7\. Navigation Integrity

Verify:

\- ☐ \`/dashboard\` remains the authenticated landing page.  
\- ☐ No additional application routes introduced.  
\- ☐ Existing navigation preserved.  
\- ☐ Logout remains available.  
\- ☐ Browser refresh preserves workspace.  
\- ☐ Returning users bypass Business Identity setup.

\*\*Result\*\*

☐ PASS

\---

\#\# 8\. Repository Compliance

Verify:

\- ☐ Repository synchronized successfully.  
\- ☐ Expected implementation committed.  
\- ☐ Existing workspace extended rather than duplicated.  
\- ☐ Repository structure preserved.  
\- ☐ Governance documentation unchanged.  
\- ☐ No unexpected files introduced.

\*\*Result\*\*

☐ PASS

\---

\#\# 9\. Deployment Verification

Verify:

\- ☐ Latest implementation published successfully.  
\- ☐ Published application reflects the approved implementation.  
\- ☐ Founder verified the published deployment.  
\- ☐ No deployment issues observed.

\*\*Result\*\*

☐ PASS

\#\# 10\. Runtime Verification

Verify:

\#\#\# Returning Business

\- ☐ Existing Business Workspace loads successfully.  
\- ☐ Existing Business Identity displayed immediately.  
\- ☐ Orientation messaging displayed.  
\- ☐ Workspace organization displayed correctly.

\#\#\# Authentication

\- ☐ Login preserved.  
\- ☐ Logout preserved.  
\- ☐ Session persistence preserved.  
\- ☐ Session restoration preserved.

\#\#\# Placeholder Behaviour

\- ☐ Placeholders display correctly.  
\- ☐ Placeholders remain informational.  
\- ☐ No backend actions triggered.  
\- ☐ No errors observed.

\*\*Result\*\*

☐ PASS

\- ☐ WhatsApp integration.  
\- ☐ Voice AI.  
\- ☐ Payments.  
\- ☐ Operational workflows.  
\- ☐ Multi-business support.

\*\*Result\*\*

☐ PASS
